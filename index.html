    Snake, Snake, Snaaakkkkeee's webpage

    Snake, Snake, Snaaakkkkeee is a snake like game with a twist. You can shoot parts of you to destroy blocks in front of you.

    This game was created for my year 12 major Software project and actually consists of a game engine and the game itself. While the assignment is over I have continued development here: RGGE-Engine The new engine includes a complete rework of 90% of the engine and amazing optimizations.
